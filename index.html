FAQ

What is a hackathon?

Just to be clear, it's not hacking into people's laptops and remotely shutting them down. Hackathons are events which usually last from 12 to 24 hours where teams of programmers, engineers, and designers work on a project. At the end of the hacking period, the projects are judged and the winners earn prizes.

What kind of experience do I need?

Anyone is welcome to attend! No experience is needed, there are various workshops offered which are offered to people ranged from beginners to experts.

What kind of things are we allowed to make?
